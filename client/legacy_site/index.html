FLOW

Fluent in Flow

Delivery Flow

Operational flow measures the efficiency of work processes and how well tasks move through the organisation. By focusing on work throughput, cycle time, and work-in-progress (WIP) limits, organisations can improve the speed and predictability of delivering value. Streamlining these processes ensures that tasks are completed efficiently and bottlenecks are minimised, leading to a smoother and more predictable workflow.

Flow Economics

Flow economics assesses the financial health of the workflow, measuring how effectively work done translates to value. Understanding the economic impact of workflow decisions helps prioritise improvements that deliver the most valuable outcomes, ensuring that resources are allocated effectively to enhance profitability.

Team Flow

Team flow evaluates how well information is communicated within the organisation, optimising the communication network and the dynamics of the organisation as a Complex Adaptive System. Emphasising information transparency, effective communication channels, and knowledge sharing ensures timely and accurate information flow to support decision-making and coordination. This approach recognises the organisation as an interconnected network rather than a rigid hierarchy or siloed teams, facilitating better collaboration, innovation and adaptability.
